Malaikat Berputar di Atas Pencakar Langit

Having finally brought .Feast to the national level after years of hard work, in late 2018 Baskara Putra sowed the seeds for what would become his solo project Hindia. Following the low-key release of "No One Will Find Me" on a compilation EP and the Tidak Ada Salju Di Sini collaboration series of satirical seasonal songs, Hindia soon set about making his solo debut album. While the outbreak of COVID-19 brought live music events to a halt in 2020, Hindia remained productive throughout the pandemic. In addition to his own music, he recorded and released two EPs with .Feast and also established Lomba Sihir - consisting of Hindia and his live backing musicians - into a full-fledged band in its own right with the release of their album Selamat Datang di Ujung Dunia. Besides pursuing his own projects, Hindia is also an in-demand collaborator who has written, produced and/or appeared on tracks by a wide array of artists ranging from legends such as Iwan Fals, Dewa Budjana and Armand Maulana, to personal heroes including RAN and Pee Wee Gaskins, and also peers such as Laze and tradeto.

Lagipula Hidup Akan Berakhir

Lagipula Hidup Akan Berakhir mengisahkan tentang warisan buruk duniawi, mulai dari krisis iklim, inflasi, politik, hingga trauma. Dalam album itu, Hindia cukup geram dengan kekacauan dunia saat ini. Lebih lanjut, Hindia menceritakan bahwa menulis lagu adalah satu-satunya cara baginya untuk menuangkan segala isi hati dan kepalanya selengkapnya..
